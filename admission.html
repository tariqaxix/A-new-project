How to Apply

Admissions are usually open during November and December. Admission forms are available in all the Aga Khan Higer Secondary Schools (Gahkuch, Hunza, Gilgit, Sherqilla). Moreover, forms are also available in different RSDUs across the GB. Once you filled the admission form, submit it to one of the mentioned schools or RSDUs. After that there will be a computer based admission test. Admission test will cover 4 subjects (Mathematics, Science, English, Urdu). You will get to know about your application status via phone within few weeks.

Eligibility Criteria

In order to qualify for admission to Grade VIII, the candidate should have:

  • Passed grade VII annual examination or be appearing in grade VII or VIII annual examination during the year of application.
  • Not have been registered with any assessment Board for a level equal to or above Secondary School Certificate Part One (SSC-I) at the time of applying for admission.
  • Be at least 12 years of age, or maximum 14 years of age on 31st March of the first academic session for Grade VIII students

Documents required

  • All applicants need a certificate from their current school stating the student's name, father's name, student's date of birth and the class he or she is studying in.
  • Attested Form B and/or attested birth certificate.
  • School leaving certificate counter signed by deputy director (to be submitted after the confirmation of admission).
